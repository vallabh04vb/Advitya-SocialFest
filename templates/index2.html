Advitya

Advitya is held every year in January. Advitya aims to foster a sense of social responsibility among young people and create awareness about fundamental human rights. Over the years, we have hosted eminent personalities like John Abraham, Kailash Kher, Sonam Kapoor, Ira Singhal, Shraddha Kapoor, Aamir Khan, Himesh Madaan, and many more.
