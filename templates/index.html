About us

Abhyuday, the social body of IIT Bombay, is a non-profit organization dedicated to sensitizing people to adopt a socially conscious outlook. Since its inception in 2014, Abhyuday has grown into one of the country's largest student-run social bodies in just 10 years. We engage the youth in impactful campaigns, competitions, and events throughout the year, addressing diverse social issues such as education, healthcare, sustainability and more.

Advitya

Advitya is Abhyuday's annual Social Fest at IIT Bombay. Rooted in the Sanskrit term meaning 'not two' or 'non-dual', Advitya literally means incomparable or unique. It serves as a profound metaphor for our collective commitment to social work and awareness. Through Advitya, we aim to break down barriers, celebrate diversity, and foster a sense of unity within our community.

About Theme

Serenade of Aspirations

This year's theme is a celebration of dreams and possibilities. Embracing the diverse melodies of aspirations, we celebrate the power of shared goals and the beauty that emerges when ambitions converge. It's not just about what we want for ourselves; it's also about using our dreams to make a difference in the world. "Serenade of Aspirations" is an opportunity to delve into the rich tapestry of ambitions that shape our community, fostering a space of positive change.
